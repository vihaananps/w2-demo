3 Great Places To Visit In New York

  1. Brooklyn Bridge

    The Brooklyn Bridge is a hybrid cable-stayed/suspension bridge in New York City, spanning the East River between the boroughs of Manhattan and Brooklyn.

  2. Times Square

    Times Square is a major commercial intersection, tourist destination, entertainment hub, and neighborhood in Midtown Manhattan, New York City. It is formed by the junction of Broadway, Seventh Avenue, and 42nd Street.

  3. Statue Of Liberty

    The Statue Of Liberty is located on Staten Island, New York. It was a gift from the French to the USA. It was dedicated on October 28, 1886. It's metal and framework was designed by Gustave Eiffel, who also designed the Eiffel tower named after him.
